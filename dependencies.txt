Jedi Academy: Renaissance uses the following libraries:

SFML 2.0 (www.sfml-dev.org)
PhysicsFS (http://icculus.org/physfs/)
zlib (http://zlib.net/)
minizip (shipped with zlib in contrib/minizip/)
Lua (http://www.lua.org)
luabind (http://www.rasterbar.com/products/luabind.html)

on Windows:
Microsoft DirectX SDK (DirectInput)
mingw-w64 (Additional windows headers for mingw)
